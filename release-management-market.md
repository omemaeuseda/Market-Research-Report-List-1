<p><strong>The Release Management Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Major Release</li><li>Minor Release</li><li>Emergency Release</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Release management encompasses various market types based on the nature and urgency of changes. A Major Release involves significant enhancements or new features, often requiring extensive testing and planning. A Minor Release introduces smaller improvements or bug fixes that enhance functionality without extensive overhauls. In contrast, an Emergency Release addresses urgent issues that need immediate action, such as critical bugs or security vulnerabilities, minimizing downtime and maintaining system integrity. Each type plays a crucial role in software lifecycle management.</p></p>
