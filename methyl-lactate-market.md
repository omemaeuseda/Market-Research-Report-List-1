<p><p>Methyl Lactate is categorized into two main markets: L-type and D-type, based on their stereoisomers. L-type, or L-lactate, is commonly used in food, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals due to its biodegradable properties and mild nature. D-type, or D-lactate, is primarily utilized in industrial applications and as a chemical intermediate. The choice between L-type and D-type often depends on specific applications, regulatory considerations, and desired performance characteristics in various formulations and processes.</p></p>
