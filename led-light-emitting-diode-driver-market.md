<p><ul><li>Constant Voltage</li><li>Constant Current</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED driver market can be classified into two main types: constant voltage and constant current drivers. Constant voltage drivers supply a fixed output voltage, making them suitable for LED applications that require consistent voltage levels across multiple LEDs, such as LED strips. In contrast, constant current drivers maintain a steady current output, ensuring uniform brightness and protection against fluctuations, which is ideal for high-power LED applications. Each type caters to specific LED configurations and performance requirements, enhancing efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
