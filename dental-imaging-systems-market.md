<p><ul><li>Intra-Oral Imaging Systems</li><li>Extra-Oral Imaging Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental imaging systems market comprises two main types: intra-oral and extra-oral imaging systems. Intra-oral imaging systems involve devices that capture images inside the mouth, such as digital sensors and intra-oral cameras, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and patient care. Extra-oral imaging systems, on the other hand, encompass broader imaging techniques like panoramic X-rays and cone-beam computed tomography (CBCT), which visualize the entire head and related structures, aiding in comprehensive treatment planning and assessment. Both play crucial roles in modern dentistry.</p></p>
