<p><p>Sterilization validation services encompass various methods to ensure medical and pharmaceutical products are free of microorganisms. Steam sterilization uses high-temperature, pressurized steam, while ethylene oxide (EtO) sterilization employs gas to penetrate materials. Electron beam radiation sterilization utilizes high-energy electrons, and gamma sterilization applies gamma rays for microbial eradication. Other methods may include chemical sterilization and dry heat. Each type has specific applications, advantages, and limitations, guiding industries in selecting the appropriate method for effective sterilization validation.</p></p>
