<p><p>The insulin preparation market is categorized into three main types: fast-acting, intermediate-acting, and long-acting insulin. Fast-acting insulin provides rapid glucose control, making it ideal for meal times. Intermediate-acting insulin offers a balanced effect, suitable for managing blood sugar levels throughout the day. Long-acting insulin ensures consistent insulin delivery, maintaining stable glucose levels over an extended period, often utilized for overnight or basal control. Each type caters to different patient needs, optimizing diabetes management and enhancing quality of life.</p></p>
