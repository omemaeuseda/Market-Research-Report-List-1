<p><p>Intrathecal drug delivery is a medical technique that involves the administration of medication directly into the cerebrospinal fluid (CSF) surrounding the spinal cord. This method allows for targeted drug delivery to the central nervous system, which can enhance the efficacy of pain management and treatment for various neurological disorders. The use of this delivery method is particularly beneficial for patients with chronic pain, cancer, multiple sclerosis, and other conditions where conventional oral or systemic therapies may be inadequate.</p><p>The Intrathecal Drug Delivery Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 12.9% during the forecast period. Key trends in the market include the development of novel drug formulations and delivery devices, enhancement in patient monitoring technologies, and a focus on personalized medicine to improve patient outcomes. <p><p>The intrathecal drug delivery market features two main types of pumps: constant rate pumps and programmable pumps.
