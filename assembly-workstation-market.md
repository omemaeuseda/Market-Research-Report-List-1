<p><p>The assembly workstation market is divided into two primary types: manual and automatic. In contrast, automatic workstations utilize machinery and robotics to streamline production, enhancing efficiency and consistency for high-volume tasks. While manual systems allow for greater customization, automatic systems reduce labor costs and increase output, catering to different industrial needs and production scales.</p></p>
