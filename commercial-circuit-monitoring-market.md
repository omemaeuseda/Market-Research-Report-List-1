<p><p>The Commercial Circuit Monitoring market encompasses two primary types: Independent Monitoring and Centralized Monitoring. Independent Monitoring involves individual systems deployed at various sites to independently track energy consumption, fault detection, and performance metrics. This allows for localized data analysis. In contrast, Centralized Monitoring aggregates data from multiple locations into a single platform, providing a holistic overview of circuit performance across a business. This type streamlines management and enhances decision-making through comprehensive insights from across the organization's operations.</p></p>
