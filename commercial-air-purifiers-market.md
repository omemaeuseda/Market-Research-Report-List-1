<p><p>The commercial air purifiers market features various technologies, including activated carbon, static electricity, ionizing, and ozone generators, among others. Ion purifiers release charged ions to neutralize airborne contaminants. Ozone generators create ozone to eliminate odors and bacteria but require caution due to ozone's potential health risks.
