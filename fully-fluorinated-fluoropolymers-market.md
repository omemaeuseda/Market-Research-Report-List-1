<p><ul><li>PTFE</li><li>FEP</li><li>PFA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fully fluorinated fluoropolymers market includes three main types: PTFE, FEP, and PFA. PTFE (Polytetrafluoroethylene) is known for its excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making it ideal for seals and gaskets. FEP (Fluorinated Ethylene Propylene) offers superior clarity and is often used for thin films and coatings. PFA (Perfluoroalkoxy alkane) combines the properties of both PTFE and FEP, providing flexibility alongside chemical resistance, which is suitable for various applications in the chemical and pharmaceutical industries.</p></p>
