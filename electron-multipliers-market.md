<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electron Multipliers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM)</li><li>Continuous-dynode multipliers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electron multipliers are devices used to amplify low levels of electrons for detection in various applications. The Channel Electron Multiplier (CEM) utilizes a series of channels to increase electron multiplication, providing high gain and sensitivity, often used in mass spectrometry and particle detection. Continuous-dynode multipliers use a continuous surface to create multiple impacts of electrons, resulting in high-level amplification across a wider range of incident electron energies. Both types serve industries like instrumentation, analytical chemistry, and space exploration.</p></p>
