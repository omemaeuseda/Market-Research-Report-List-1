<p><p>The low-frequency speaker market encompasses various types, primarily including woofers, midwoofers, and subwoofers. Woofers handle low to mid-range frequencies, providing rich sound and depth to music and audio. Midwoofers bridge the gap between woofers and tweeters, delivering clearer mids while still offering some low-frequency response. Subwoofers specialize in reproducing deep bass sounds, enhancing the overall audio experience, particularly in home theater systems and music production. Each type serves a unique purpose in delivering high-quality sound across different applications.</p></p>
