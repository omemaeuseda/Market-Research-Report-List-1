Sargramostim, a granulocyte-macrophage colony-stimulating factor (GM-CSF), enhances both neutrophil and macrophage production, providing a broader immune response. These therapies are crucial for managing neutropenia in patients undergoing chemotherapy or with certain hematological disorders.</p></p>
