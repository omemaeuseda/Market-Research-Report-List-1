<p><p>The POC Glycosylated Hemoglobin Analyzers market comprises various types, including immunoassay, boronate affinity, and others. Immunoassay methods utilize antibodies to detect and quantify glycosylated hemoglobin, offering high specificity. Boronate affinity techniques leverage the unique binding properties of boronic acids to isolate hemoglobin variants, providing accurate measurements. Other methods may include chromatography and spectrophotometry, enhancing flexibility in testing approaches. Each type serves to support diabetes monitoring and management, catering to different clinical settings and requirements.</p></p>
