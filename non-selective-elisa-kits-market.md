<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Non-Selective ELISA Kits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct ELISA</li><li>Sandwich ELISA</li><li>Indirect ELISA</li><li>Competitive ELISA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Selective ELISA Kits market includes various types: </p><p>1. **Direct ELISA** detects antigens directly attached to a solid surface using enzyme-linked antibodies.</p><p>2. **Sandwich ELISA** captures the antigen between two antibodies, enhancing specificity and sensitivity.</p><p>3. **Indirect ELISA** involves a primary antibody binding to the antigen and a secondary enzyme-linked antibody for detection.</p><p>4. **Competitive ELISA** quantifies antigen levels by measuring the inhibition of binding between a sample antigen and a labeled antigen. Each method serves distinct research and diagnostic needs.</p></p>
