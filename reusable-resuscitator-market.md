<p><ul><li>Self-inflating Resuscitator</li><li>Flow-inflating Resuscitator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reusable resuscitator market primarily comprises two types: self-inflating resuscitators and flow-inflating resuscitators. Self-inflating resuscitators automatically refill with air after each compression, making them user-friendly and suitable for emergency situations. On the other hand, flow-inflating resuscitators require a continuous gas supply, allowing for precise control over ventilation pressure and volume. Both types are essential in healthcare settings for delivering positive pressure ventilation during respiratory emergencies, ensuring adequate oxygenation and support for patients in critical conditions.</p></p>
