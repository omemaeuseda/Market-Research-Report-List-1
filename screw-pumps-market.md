<p><strong>The Screw Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Screw Pump</li><li>Twin-Screw Pump</li><li>Multiple Screw Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The screw pumps market is categorized into three main types: single screw, twin-screw, and multiple screw pumps. Single screw pumps use a single helical rotor for fluid transfer, ideal for low-viscosity liquids. Twin-screw pumps employ two parallel rotors, offering higher efficiency and handling viscous fluids. Multiple screw pumps consist of three or more screws, providing smooth and continuous flow with high pressure, making them suitable for demanding applications in industries like oil and gas, food processing, and chemicals.</p></p>
