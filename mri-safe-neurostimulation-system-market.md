<p><p>The MRI-Safe Neurostimulation System market comprises two main types: implantable and transcutaneous systems. Implantable neurostimulation devices are surgically placed within the body to deliver targeted stimulation directly to neural tissues, often used for chronic pain management or neurological disorders. Transcutaneous systems, on the other hand, apply stimulation through the skin, utilizing non-invasive techniques to target peripheral nerves. Both types prioritize safety and compatibility with magnetic resonance imaging, ensuring patients can undergo MRI scans without risk.</p></p>
