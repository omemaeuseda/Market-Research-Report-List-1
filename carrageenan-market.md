<p><strong>The Carrageenan Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Kappa Carrageenan</li><li>Iota Carrageenan</li><li>Lambda Carrageenan</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The carrageenan market is categorized into three main types: Kappa, Iota, and Lambda. Kappa carrageenan is primarily used for gelling, thickening, and stabilizing in dairy products and desserts. Iota carrageenan offers a softer gel and is often found in dairy alternatives and soups. Lambda carrageenan, known for its thickening properties, does not gel and is typically used in products like sauces and dressings. Each type serves distinct functions in food and industrial applications, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
