<p><p>The speech recognition software market can be categorized into three main types: </p><p>1. **Isolated Word Recognition**: This technology processes individual words spoken one at a time, ideal for command systems or specific applications where clear distinct inputs are necessary.</p><p>2. **Keyword Spotting**: This approach listens for specific keywords within continuous speech, enabling applications like voice-activated assistants to function effectively.</p><p>3. **Continuous Speech Recognition**: This sophisticated system understands and transcribes natural, flowing speech, making it suitable for dictation and interactive voice response systems.</p></p>
