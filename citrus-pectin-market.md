<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Citrus Pectin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Methoxyl Pectin (DM: More Than 50)</li><li>Low Methoxyl Pectin (DM: Less Than 50)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Citrus Pectin market is primarily divided into two types based on methoxyl content: High Methoxyl Pectin (HMP) and Low Methoxyl Pectin (LMP). HMP, characterized by a degree of methoxyl (DM) greater than 50, is commonly used in jams, jellies, and confectionery due to its gelling properties. In contrast, LMP, with a DM of less than 50, is favored in low-sugar products and dairy applications for its ability to gel at lower sugar concentrations, thus offering versatility in food formulations.</p></p>
