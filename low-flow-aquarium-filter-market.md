<p><p>The low flow aquarium filter market consists of three main types: mechanical, biological, and chemical filters. Mechanical filters physically remove debris and particulate matter from the water, ensuring clarity. Biological filters support beneficial bacteria that break down toxic ammonia and nitrites, promoting a healthy ecosystem. Chemical filters utilize substances like activated carbon to remove impurities and odors, enhancing water quality. Together, these filters ensure optimal conditions for aquatic life while catering to varying tank sizes and fish species.</p></p>
