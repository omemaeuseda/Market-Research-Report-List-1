<p><p>In the 3D hydrogels in cell culture market, two primary types are scaffold-free and scaffold-based systems. Scaffold-free hydrogels enable cells to self-assemble into tissues without a physical support structure, promoting natural cell behavior and interaction. In contrast, scaffold-based hydrogels provide a supportive matrix for cell attachment, proliferation, and differentiation, mimicking the extracellular matrix. Both types facilitate advanced tissue engineering and regenerative medicine applications, enhancing cell growth and functionality in laboratory settings.</p></p>
