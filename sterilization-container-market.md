<p><strong>The Sterilization Container Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Valve Type</li><li>Filter Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The sterilization container market encompasses two primary types: valve-type and filter-type containers. Valve-type containers feature built-in valves that allow for controlled airflow during sterilization, optimizing the process and reducing contamination risks. On the other hand, filter-type containers utilize specialized filters that permit gas exchange while preventing the entry of contaminants. Both types effectively maintain sterility, but they cater to different procedural needs in medical and surgical environments, ensuring safe storage and transportation of sterilized instruments and equipment.</p></p>
