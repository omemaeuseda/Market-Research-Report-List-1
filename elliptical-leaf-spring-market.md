<p><strong>The Elliptical Leaf Spring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multi-leaf Spring</li><li>Mono-leaf Spring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elliptical leaf spring market is categorized into two main types: multi-leaf springs and mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs consist of several layered leafs stacked together, providing enhanced strength, stability, and load-bearing capacity, commonly used in heavy-duty applications like trucks and trailers. In contrast, mono-leaf springs utilize a single, broad leaf, promoting lighter weight and improved flexibility, making them ideal for light vehicles and applications requiring less rigidity. Both types serve crucial roles in suspension systems, optimizing performance and comfort.</p></p>
