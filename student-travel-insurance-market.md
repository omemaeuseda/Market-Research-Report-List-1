<p><p>The student travel insurance market consists primarily of two types: Single Trip Coverage and Annual Multi-Trip Coverage. Single Trip Coverage is designed for students traveling for a specific period, providing protection for that single journey, including medical emergencies and trip cancellations. In contrast, Annual Multi-Trip Coverage offers a more flexible option for students who plan multiple trips within a year, covering each trip for a predetermined duration. This type provides convenience and cost-effectiveness for frequent travelers, ensuring ongoing protection.</p></p>
