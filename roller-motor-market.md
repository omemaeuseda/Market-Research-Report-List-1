<p><strong>The Roller Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electric</li><li>Pneumatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roller motor market is primarily divided into two types: electric and pneumatic. Electric roller motors utilize electrical energy to drive roller mechanisms, offering precise control, efficiency, and low maintenance. They are commonly used in applications requiring consistent speed and torque. Pneumatic roller motors, on the other hand, operate using compressed air, providing high power output for heavy-duty tasks. They are ideal for environments where electrical sources may be limited or hazardous, offering flexibility and reliability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
