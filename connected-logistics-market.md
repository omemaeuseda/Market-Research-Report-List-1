<p><p>The Connected Logistics market encompasses three key types: Device Management, Application Management, and Connectivity Management. Device Management focuses on tracking and controlling physical assets through IoT devices, ensuring optimal performance and maintenance. Application Management involves the software solutions that analyze logistics data for decision-making and operational efficiency. Connectivity Management oversees the communication networks, ensuring reliable data transmission between devices, applications, and users. Together, these components enhance supply chain visibility, streamline operations, and improve overall logistics performance.</p></p>
